# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

LinguaMesh contributors commit to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community. Discuss ideas and changes on their technical merits, communicate constructively, and respect language, cultural, and accessibility differences.

## Expected behavior

- Give actionable, professional feedback.
- Welcome questions and differing levels of experience.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing personal information.
- Attribute others' work and follow project licenses.
- De-escalate conflict and accept maintainers' moderation decisions.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, sustained disruption, and deliberate exposure of private information are unacceptable.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's configured contact channel. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
